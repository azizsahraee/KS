Welcome to KS Genuine Quality Parts

Serving Afghanistan since 2007 with Quality and Trust

At KS, a name inspired by the founders Kabir and Shafiq, we have been dedicated to providing top-quality vehicle spare parts across Afghanistan since 2007. Our journey began with importing reliable spare parts for Russian vehicles like Kamaz and Kraz from China, India, and Pakistan.

By 2016, we launched our own brand, expanding to include high-quality spare parts for German trucks such as Benz and MAN, manufactured with precision in China. Later, we further broadened our range to Japanese vehicles like Mazda and Hino, ensuring our customers receive only the best in durability and performance.
